About Us

WillowBrook is a small private non-profit school establish in 1990 by James Willowbrook. At the beginning, we only had approximately 500 students with about 60 faculty members. Throughout the years, we have developed, growed and become one of the best schools in America. Our students are taught using fun, interactive methods that we sucessfully develop through trial and error. We prepared them well so that they can be ready to tackle any tasks that await them at higher level classes once they graduate from Willowbrook. We are proud of our students and what we do. We are dedicated, commited, and willing to give it our best for every generation that walk through our door.
